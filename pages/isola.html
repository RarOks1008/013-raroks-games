Phase 1: Moving pieces

Move one's piece to a neighboring (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) position that contains a square but not the opponent's piece.

Phase 2: Removing square

Players then remove the unoccupied place on which player's piece was before moving it.

Phase 3: Ending

The player who can not make any move loses the game.
